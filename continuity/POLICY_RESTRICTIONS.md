# Policy-Restricted / Declined Features

These items were requested during development and were intentionally not implemented due to policy restrictions. Keep this list with the continuity package so the next session does not accidentally re-open them.

## Declined features

1. IMSI/TMSI extraction or IMSI-catcher behavior.
2. SMS capture.
3. cellular user-plane/content interception.
4. subscriber-identity interception.
5. replay, retransmit, injection, cloning, or active attack workflows.
6. decryption or cracking of encrypted traffic.
7. recovery of third-party communication payloads from satellite or cellular links.

## Safe boundary retained in project direction

1. passive RF metadata collection
2. passive Wi-Fi/Bluetooth observation
3. safe decoder process control and dependency management
4. non-content coordinate/telemetry plotting where policy permits
